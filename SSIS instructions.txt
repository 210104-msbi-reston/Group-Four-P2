----How to use Travel Agency SSIS----
1. you must have the Travel Agency - DW ver2 excel document in a folder called project2Data this folder must be located at the root of C drive

2. you must have the DW tables on your server (you can do this by running the SQL located in the TravelAgencyDWSQL.sql file)

3. you need to change the connection manager from SLEDGEHAMMER.TravelAgencyDW to YOURSERVERNAME.TravelAgencyDW
